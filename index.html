About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineer with a strong background in aerospace propulsion, technical documentation, and precision product development. At LCI Aero, I lead day-to-day engineering operations, ensuring high-quality technical output, regulatory compliance, efficient workflows, and clear cross-department communication. My role includes repair development, inspection support, technical reporting, and tooling and process development across multiple jet engine platforms, including CFM56, LEAP, V2500, and CF34.

Beyond aerospace, I founded Tabash Design Works, a mechanical design studio specializing in reverse engineering, prototyping, and custom hardware development. My work spans vehicle adapters and restoration components to product mockups, custom tools, and field-tested solutions for motorsport, manufacturing, and consumer applications. I take a hands-on, iterative approach to improving manufacturability, recreating legacy parts in CAD, and developing functional prototypes from concept to execution.

My skill set includes 3D CAD, reverse engineering, additive manufacturing, Arduino integration, tooling development, internal combustion and turbine engines, and technical documentation. I’m always open to new projects, collaborations, and freelance opportunities.
