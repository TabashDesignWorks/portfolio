Project Information

  • Name: Portable Monitor V1
  • Category: Upcycling
  • Client: SPQR Group LLC.
  • Project date: 23 January, 2024

Details:

For a cost-conscious client seeking a practical solution, we embarked on a project aimed at repurposing an old broken laptop with a functional LCD screen into a fully operational portable monitor. Understanding the importance of budget constraints, our approach involved sourcing a compatible LCD controller board tailored to the existing LCD screen. Once secured, our team meticulously measured the dimensions of the components to craft a bespoke mounting system using SolidWorks, ensuring seamless integration and optimal functionality. Leveraging 3D printing technology, we materialized the design into a robust housing, meticulously engineered to house the LCD controller board securely. The final assembly seamlessly integrated the housing with the newly repurposed monitor, delivering a cost-effective yet high-quality solution that exceeded the client's expectations.
