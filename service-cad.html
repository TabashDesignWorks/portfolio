3D Printing & CAD

Our CAD and 3D printing services provide precise models and components built for aerospace, defense, and product development. We specialize in manufacturing-ready CAD, STL export, and 3D print prototyping.

Whether you’re working from a napkin sketch or legacy part, we transform ideas into technical drawings and manufacturable geometry using tools like SolidWorks and Fusion 360.

What We Deliver:

  • High-accuracy CAD modeling of parts and assemblies
  • 3D printing files (STL, OBJ) and fitment test prints
  • Detailed mechanical drawings for suppliers
  • Design iterations with manufacturability in mind
